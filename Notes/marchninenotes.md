Since we’re going deeper into the water and collecting and selling all the fish, the other component is gonna be a clicker area on the shore. So define a beach area that the user will return to frequently. This is gonna be on the beach and your clicking water, that’s the resource, the point of the game which is now called ‘buy the sea’ a pun meaning ur by the sea and also buying up the whole ocean. Anyway, you click on the water and gain one drop of water, as you click more and more you can get buildings like manual labor, scooping machines, draining machines, and it gets crazier and crazier. The user starts the game on the shore and after getting 100 water drops can descend into the coast where the underwater game starts. There should be a metric to convert water into the currency in game, the conversion starts at a 10 water to 1 gold currency. The player needs to be able to easy return to the shore and then back into the water. The story here is you’re an evil corporation in the year 2383 and you’ve begun the process of draining the sea of all the water and fishes. The first three things you can buy which passively generate water are: dropper, cup, sponge, pipe.
